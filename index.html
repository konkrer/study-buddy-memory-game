How to play:

Pick a topic then start the game.

You can flip one card at a time then flip it back over. Flip two cards over at the same time when you think you can pair a question with its answer. If you are correct your points remain at 100, if you are wrong you will lose ten points.

When you have paired all question cards with their answer card or time runs out your final score will be calculated by multiplying your points by the timer value. The quicker you finish the higher score you get.
